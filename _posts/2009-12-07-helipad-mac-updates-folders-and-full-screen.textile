"Helipad Mac":http://helipadapp.com/mac.html now has folders and full screen support.

To edit folders, select a document then press the _Folders_ button.  Add a folder, double-click it to rename, them click the checkbox to add the document to the folder.

Folders can be renamed and deleted.

Full screen view is available in the _Window_ menu or by pressing cmd-shift-F.  Press cmd-shift-F to exit full screen.
