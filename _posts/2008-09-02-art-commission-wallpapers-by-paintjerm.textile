Our most recent "commission":http://flickr.com/photos/helicoid/collections/72157607055315472/ is by Paintjerm, an artist based in the Midlands, UK.  These wallpapers were created by first sketching out the design, and then producing black and white vectors.

!http://farm4.static.flickr.com/3078/2818398896_4e4bbe8141_m.jpg!:http://www.flickr.com/photos/helicoid/2818398896/

h3. About the artist

Paintjerm is a creative chap based in the Midlands (UK) working in a variety of media.  Currently working on illustration, animation, graphic design and art workshops.

bq. At home working in a box room on his computer or painting a 20ft picture of a goat in an abandoned warehouse.

To get in touch with Paintjerm, email paintjerm@googlemail.com or "contact us":http://helicoid.net/contact

h3. About our commissions

We're spending our marketing budget on commissioning work from artists!  If you're an artist and you'd like to contribute (and get paid for your work of course), "get in touch":http://helicoid.net/contact.  If you're based in London we might take you out for a beer.
