"Tiktrac":http://tiktrac.com now supports data imports from CSV files.  This allows you to "import time sheet data":http://tiktrac.com/import_and_export from spreadsheets straight into Tiktrac.

Each sheet now has an *Import* tab, which takes you through the steps to import your data.
